Book

This book gathers years of travels, encounters, and transformations. Photographs, drawings, texts, and documents intertwine to narrate a human and artistic journey across Africa, Iceland, Greenland, and other places around the world.

It was born from the desire to share not only images, but also the stories, relationships, and lived experiences that shaped the CIMA Project. Each chapter is a crossing — a fragment of life that becomes art.

The volume is currently in development and will be presented through exhibitions, conferences, and public events. This page will be updated with previews, excerpts, and publication details.

Giorgio Sedda – CIMA Project
