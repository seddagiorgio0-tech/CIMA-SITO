Biography

Giorgio Sedda is an artist, traveler, and visual researcher who builds his work through direct experience within the communities he encounters. His path brings together photography, drawing, writing, and educational projects, transforming each journey into a human and creative archive.

He has worked in Africa, Iceland, Greenland, and numerous European contexts, collaborating with cultural institutions, schools, public organizations, and research centers. His works emerge from contact with people, from shared daily life, and from a respectful observation of places.

Through the CIMA Project – Crossing Into My Art – Sedda develops a language that blends documentation and visual poetry, inviting the public to enter his world as it transforms.
