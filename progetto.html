GIORGIO SEDDA

CIMA Project

CIMA – Crossing Into My Art is a project born from travel, encounters, and transformation. Every experience lived within distant communities becomes artistic material, documentation, storytelling, and an opportunity for cultural exchange.

The project brings together photography, drawing, writing, lectures, and exhibitions, creating a bridge between places, people, and languages. Each stage is a crossing: geographical, human, and creative.

CIMA is an invitation to enter my world as it transforms, to follow the traces left by journeys, relationships, and the stories I encounter along the way.

Giorgio Sedda – CIMA Project
